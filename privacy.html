Facebook SDK — Data Collection and IDFA

The Application integrates the Facebook SDK, which may collect device identifiers including the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS. The Facebook SDK uses this data for ad attribution, measuring ad performance, and delivering personalized ads. It may also collect usage analytics and purchase events to understand app engagement. IDFA collection follows Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework—we will request your permission before tracking across apps and websites. You can manage or revoke this permission in your device Settings at any time.

PostHog Analytics — Data Collection

PostHog Analytics helps us understand how users interact with the Application. It collects screen views, user behavior (such as button clicks and feature usage), and general device information (device type, OS version). This data is used in aggregated form to improve the app, prioritize features, and fix issues. PostHog does not typically link this data to personally identifiable information; analytics are processed in a privacy-preserving manner.

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — Data Collection

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) is used to display advertisements in the Application. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. This data is used for ad targeting and is generally not linked to your identity. You can control personalized advertising through your device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).
